Upcoming Events

PGL Bucharest 2025

PGL Bucharest 2025 marks a grand return of Counter-Strike 2's elite competition to Eastern Europe. Featuring the top 24 teams from around the globe, this Major will be held in the iconic Polyvalent Hall, Bucharest. Fans can expect breathtaking matches, surprise upsets, and the thrilling culmination of months of intense qualifiers. Witness the very best of Counter-Strike 2 as they battle for the lion's share of a $2 million prize pool and the coveted Major trophy.

Date: June 14 - June 23, 2025

Location: Bucharest, Romania

Prize Pool: $2,000,000 USD

IEM Melbourne 2025

IEM returns to Australia with IEM Melbourne 2025, bringing top-tier Counter-Strike 2 action to the iconic Rod Laver Arena. A total of 16 teams from across the globe will face off in a high-octane competition for a million-dollar prize pool. This event promises thrilling matches, enthusiastic crowds, and an electric atmosphere as teams compete for the IEM trophy and essential ESL Pro Tour points.

Date: April 12 – April 27, 2025

Location: Online (Regional Divisions)

Prize Pool: $750,000 USD

Past Events

PGL Astana 2025 Europe Closed Qualifier

The PGL Astana 2025 Europe Closed Qualifier was an intense online battleground where the best teams from Europe clashed for a chance to qualify for the prestigious PGL Astana Major. Spanning four days of high-stakes competition, this tournament featured rising contenders and established giants vying for a place at the main event. With $50,000 on the line and only the strongest advancing, the matches were a showcase of skill, strategy, and resilience.

Date: March 15 – March 18, 2025

Location: Online (Europe)

Prize Pool: $50,000 USD

BLAST Open Lisbon 2025

Held at the Altice Arena, BLAST Open Lisbon 2025 brought top-tier Counter-Strike 2 action to the heart of Portugal. Featuring 12 invited teams and 4 qualified challengers, the event delivered unforgettable moments of tactical brilliance and clutch performances. With a prize pool of $300,000, this tournament served as a crucial stepping stone for teams aiming to establish themselves as serious contenders in the 2025 season.

Date: February 22 – February 25, 2025

Location: Lisbon, Portugal

Prize Pool: $300,000 USD
